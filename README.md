## digiCard
You ever tried to get your friend a card while in a rush? 
Or do you just want to give your friend a little surprise?
Digicard lets you make cards for people on the go, or at your desktop to put a smile on their face. 
No matter the ocassion, digicard is there for you and your circle, bringing your messages with style.
